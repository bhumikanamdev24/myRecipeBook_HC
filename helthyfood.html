Sprouts Salad

Ingredients: Moong sprouts (1 cup), Onion, Tomato, Cucumber, Lemon juice, Salt, Pepper.

Calories: ~120 kcal/bowl • Prep: 10 min

Steps:
Sprouts ko achchhe se wash karo.
Onion, tomato aur cucumber finely chop karo.
Sprouts ke saath sabzi mix karo.
Salt, pepper aur lemon juice daal kar serve karo.

Oats Upma

Ingredients: Oats (1 cup), Onion, Green chili, Curry leaves, Vegetables (carrot, beans, peas), Oil, Mustard seeds.

Calories: ~200 kcal/serving • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Oats ko dry roast karo aur side me rakho.
Pan me oil garam karke mustard seeds, curry leaves aur green chili daalo.
Vegetables aur onion saute karo.
Water daal kar boil hone do, phir oats add karke cook karo.
Soft hone par serve karo.

Quinoa Salad

Olive oil drizzle karke serve karo.

Vegetable Soup

Ingredients: Carrot, Beans, Cabbage, Tomato, Onion, Garlic, Salt, Pepper, Olive oil.

Ragi Dosa

Ingredients: Ragi flour (1 cup), Rice flour (½ cup), Curd, Onion, Green chili, Curry leaves, Salt.

Calories: ~130 kcal/dosa • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Ragi flour, rice flour aur curd ko mix karke smooth batter banao.
Batter me onion, green chili aur curry leaves daal do.
Tawa garam karke thin dosa spread karo.
Thoda oil daal kar crispy hone tak cook karo.
Chutney ya sambhar ke saath serve karo.

Brown Rice Pulao

Ingredients: Brown rice (1 cup), Mixed vegetables, Onion, Spices, Oil, Salt.

Calories: ~210 kcal/serving • Prep: 30 min

Steps:
Brown rice ko 20 min soak karke cook kar lo.
Pan me oil dal kar onion aur masale bhuno.
Vegetables add karke thoda saute karo.
Cooked rice daal kar sab mix karo aur 5 min steam do.
Serve garma garam.

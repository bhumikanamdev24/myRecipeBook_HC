Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer (250g), Butter (2 tbsp), Tomato (3), Cream (3 tbsp), Garam masala, Onion, Ginger-garlic paste, Salt.

Calories: ~350 kcal/serving • Prep: 30–35 min

Steps:
Paneer ko cubes me kaat lo; agar chaho halka fry karlo.
Tomatoes + onion ko bhune aur blender se smooth paste bana lo.
Pan me butter garam karo, ginger-garlic daalo, bhuno.
Tomato-onion paste add karo, masale (garam masala, salt) daal kar 8–10 min pakao.
Cream aur thoda pani daal kar gravy set karo, paneer add karke 5 min halki aanch par simmer karo.
Fresh cream/kasuri methi se garnish karke serve karo.

Dal Tadka

Ingredients: Toor/masoor dal (1 cup), Ghee (1 tbsp), Garlic, Tomato, Cumin, Turmeric, Red chilli, Salt.

Calories: ~220 kcal/serving • Prep: 25–30 min

Steps:
Dal ko dhokar 2-3 cups paani aur haldi ke saath pressure cook karo till soft.
Pan me ghee garam karo; jeera daalo, phir kali mirch/lasun fry karo.
Finely chopped tomato add karo aur soft hone tak bhuno.
Cooked dal pan me daal kar consistency set karo, zarurat ho to pani aur namak adjust karo.
Tadka ke liye ghee me rai, jeera, laal mirch aur hing ka tadka bana ke upar daal do.
Hara dhaniya se garnish karke serve karo.

Chole Bhature

Ingredients (chole): Kabuli chana (1 cup), Onion, Tomato, Chole masala, Ginger-garlic;
(bhature): Maida, Yogurt, Baking powder, Salt.

Calories: ~600 kcal/plate • Prep: ~1 hr (soak + cook)

Steps:
Chane ko raat bhar bhigo do. Pressure cook karke soft karo.
Pan me oil, jeera, chopped onion bhuno; ginger-garlic aur tomatoes add karo.
Chole masala, namak daal kar thoda bhuno; cooked chane add karke 10–15 min dum pe pakao.
Bhature ke liye maida + dahi + baking powder + namak se soft dough banake 30 min rest karao.
Dough se balls le ke belan se roll karo aur garam tel me deep fry karo till puff ho jaye.
Garam chole aur bhature serve karo—achar/onion ke saath.

Rajma Chawal

Ingredients: Rajma (1 cup), Onion, Tomato, Rice (1 cup), Garam masala, Ginger-garlic.

Calories: ~450 kcal/serving • Prep: ~1 hr

Steps:
Rajma raat bhar bhigo kar pressure cook karo until soft.
Pan me oil, chopped onion bhuno; ginger-garlic paste aur tomato add karo.
Masale (turmeric, red chilli, garam masala) daal kar gravy banao.
Cooked rajma gravy me daal ke 10–15 min dhimi aanch pe pakao; pani adjust karo.
Rice separately jeera + thoda oil me bhun kar paani add karke pakao.
Rajma chawal garam garam serve karo.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Dosa batter (ready), Potato (2–3), Mustard seeds, Curry leaves, Green chilli, Turmeric, Onion.

Calories: ~300 kcal/dosa • Prep: 30–40 min (batter ready assumed)

Steps:
Sabse pehle aloo boil kar ke peel aur mash karo.
Pan me oil, rai, curry leaves, chopped onion aur green chilli bhuno; haldi aur mashed aloo mix karo; namak aur thoda lemon daal kar mix.
Tawa garam karo, thoda oil lagao, batter thoda patla kar ke circular spread karo.
Dosa crispy hone par aloo masala beech me rakho aur fold kar do.
Coconut chutney/sambar ke saath serve karo.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients: Mixed veggies (potato, peas, capsicum, cauliflower), Pav masala, Butter, Tomatoes, Pav.

Calories: ~420 kcal/serving • Prep: 40 min

Steps:
Sab vegetables ko boil ya pressure cook karke mash kar lo.
Pan me butter/ghee, chopped onion bhuno; tomato add karke masala bhuno.
Pav masala aur mashed veggies add karo; thoda pani daal ke 10–12 min pakao.
Consistency thick rakhna—achhi tarah mash karo (masher use karo).
Pav ko butter me shallow fry karke serve karo.
Lemon aur finely chopped onion saath serve karo.

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients: Wheat flour (atta), Boiled potato (2), Coriander, Green chilli, Ghee/Butter.

Calories: ~300–350 kcal/paratha • Prep: 30–35 min

Steps:
Atta se soft dough banake 15–20 min rest karao.
Boiled aloo mash karo; chopped coriander, mirch, salt aur jeera powder mix karo.
Dough ka ball leke bel kar stuffing rakh kar seal karo aur phir belan se paratha bel lo.
Tawa garam karke thoda ghee laga ke dono taraf golden brown tak sekho.
Serve with dahi/achar/butter.

Baingan Bharta

Ingredients: Eggplant (large), Onion, Tomato, Garlic, Mustard oil, Coriander.

Calories: ~180 kcal/serving • Prep: 35–40 min

Steps:
Baingan ko direct flame/parchment oven pe roast karo jab tak skin char aur pulp soft ho jaye.
Thanda hone par peel kar pulp ko finely mash karo.
Pan me oil, chopped onion + garlic bhuno; tomatoes add karke masala banao.
Mashed baingan add karo, thoda namak, haldi, mirch daal ke 8–10 min pakao.
Hara dhaniya daal kar serve karo.

Veg Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, potato), Biryani masala, Yogurt, Onion, Saffron (optional).

Calories: ~450–500 kcal/serving • Prep: 45–60 min

Steps:
Rice ko 70% tak parboil karo (salt, bay leaf).
Pan me ghee, sliced onions fry karke golden karo (birista).
Veg bhuno, biryani masala aur dahi add karke thoda gravy banao.
Layering: handi me pehle thoda veg mixture, phir rice, phir birista aur saffron milk; layer repeat karo.
Dum ke liye handi ko tight bandh kar 15–20 min low flame pe rakh do (dum).
Halke se fluff karke serve karo.

Palak Paneer

Ingredients: Spinach (250g), Paneer, Onion, Garlic, Cream (optional).

Calories: ~320 kcal/serving • Prep: 30–35 min

Steps:
Palak ko blanch karke thanda kar ke blender me smooth puree bana lo.
Pan me oil/ghee, chopped onion + garlic bhuno.
Palak puree daal kar 5–7 min pakao, namak aur garam masala add karo.
Paneer cubes lightly fry karke palak me add karo; cream daal kar 2 min simmer karo.
Serve with roti/naan.

Sambar Idli

View Recipe

Ingredients (sambar): Toor dal, Tamarind, Mixed veggies (drumstick, carrot), Sambar masala;
(idli): Idli batter ready.

Calories: ~250–300 kcal / 2 idlis + sambar • Prep: 30–45 min (batter ready)

Steps:

  1. Toor dal ko cook karke mash karo.
  2. Pan me oil, mustard, curry leaves temper karo; chopped veggies add karo aur thoda pakao.
  3. Tamarind pulp + sambar masala aur cooked dal add karo; 10–15 min simmer.
  4. Idli batter se idli steam karo (10–12 min).
  5. Garam sambar ke saath idli serve karo.

Veg Pulao

View Recipe

Ingredients: Basmati rice, Mixed veg, Whole spices (clove, bay, cinnamon), Ghee/oil.

Calories: ~350 kcal/serving • Prep: 30–35 min

Steps:

  1. Ghee me whole spices bhuno, sliced onions daal ke golden karo.
  2. Mixed veg add karo, thoda bhuno; rice daal kar 2 min toss karo.
  3. Paani (rice:water = 1:2 approx) add karo, namak daal ke lid se pakao.
  4. Steam se 5 min rest kar ke fluff karo aur serve karo.

Kadhi Pakora

View Recipe

Ingredients: Besan (for kadhi + pakora), Yogurt, Onion (for pakora), Mustard seeds, Curry leaves.

Calories: ~380 kcal/serving • Prep: 40–50 min

Steps:

  1. Pakora batter: besan + sliced onion + spices, thoda pani se thick batter banao; small pakore fry karo.
  2. Kadhi ke liye dahi aur besan ko mix kar ke smooth batter banalo.
  3. Pan me oil, mustard, curry leaves tadka, phir kadhi batter daal kar continuously stir karo till boil.
  4. Simmer pe 15–20 min pakao till raw besan smell chala jaye.
  5. Fried pakore kadhi me daal ke 5 min aur cook karo.
  6. Serve with chawal.

Stuffed Capsicum

View Recipe

Ingredients: Large capsicums (4), Spiced potato/veg stuffing (boiled potato + spices), Onion, Tomato, Cheese (optional).

Calories: ~220–300 kcal/one stuffed capsicum • Prep: 35–45 min

Steps:

  1. Capsicum ke tops cut karke seeds nikaal do.
  2. Stuffing banane ke liye boiled mashed potato + sautéed onion, tomato, spices mix karo.
  3. Capsicums me stuffing bhar do; upar cheese sprinkle karo (optional).
  4. Oven me 180°C par 15–20 min bake karo ya pan me thoda tel dal kar covered cook karo till soft.
  5. Serve hot.

Malai Kofta

View Recipe

Ingredients: Paneer + potato for kofta, Cashew paste, Tomato + cream gravy, Cardamom.

Calories: ~420 kcal/serving • Prep: 45–50 min

Steps:

  1. Koftas ke liye paneer + boiled potato mash karke small balls banake lightly fry karo.
  2. Gravy: onion-tomato bhune, cashew paste add karo aur smooth gravy banao.
  3. Gravy me cream aur spices daal kar thick karo.
  4. Fry ki hui koftas ko gravy me serve karte waqt daalo (dum pe zyada mat pakao warna toot sakte hain).
  5. Garnish with cream / crushed cardamom.

Jeera Rice

View Recipe

Ingredients: Basmati rice, Cumin seeds (jeera), Ghee, Bay leaf.

Calories: ~200 kcal/serving • Prep: 20–25 min

Steps:

  1. Basmati rice ko wash karke 20 min soak karo.
  2. Pan me ghee garam karo, jeera aur bay leaf daal kar tadka lagao.
  3. Soaked rice daal kar 1 min bhuno, phir paani + namak daal kar lid close karo.
  4. Medium flame par 10–12 min cook karo jab tak rice fluffy ho jaye.
  5. Serve hot with curry / dal.

Poori Sabzi

View Recipe

Ingredients (poori): Wheat flour, Oil; (sabzi): Potato, Tomato, Spices (turmeric, garam masala).

Calories: ~450–500 kcal/plate • Prep: 40–45 min

Steps:

  1. Poori dough: atta + thoda oil + namak se tight dough banao, rest 10 min.
  2. Dough se small balls le kar bel ke discs bana lo.
  3. Deep fry in hot oil till puff aur golden.

  5. Poori garam-garam sabzi ke saath serve karo.

Bhindi Masala

View Recipe

Ingredients: Bhindi (okra 300g), Onion, Tomato, Coriander, Spices (turmeric, coriander powder).

Calories: ~160 kcal/serving • Prep: 25–30 min

Steps:

  1. Bhindi ko dhoke pocha karo (dry hone chahiye), phir slices karo.
  2. Pan me thoda oil garam kar ke bhindi ko separately roast/cook karo till crisp.
  3. Dusre pan me onion-tomato bhuno, masale add karo.
  4. Roast ki hui bhindi add kar ke 5–7 min mix karo.
  5. Hara dhaniya se garnish kar ke serve karo.

Shahi Paneer

View Recipe

Ingredients: Paneer, Cream, Cashew paste, Tomato-onion gravy, Cardamom, Sugar pinch.

Calories: ~420 kcal/serving • Prep: 35–40 min

Steps:

  1. Onion aur tomato bhune aur blender se smooth paste bana lo (saath me thoda cashew paste add karo).
  2. Pan me ghee, paste daal kar cook karo; thoda sugar aur garam masala add karo for royal taste.
  3. Paneer cubes halka fry karke gravy me add karo.
  4. Cream daal kar 5 min simmer karo; cardamom powder sprinkle karo.
  5. Serve with naan/roti.

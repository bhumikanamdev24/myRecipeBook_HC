Mango Pickle

Ingredients: Raw mangoes (500g), Mustard oil (½ cup), Salt (3 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tbsp), Fenugreek seeds (1 tbsp).

Calories: ~40 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Wash and cut raw mangoes into small pieces.
Sun-dry slightly to remove moisture.
Roast fenugreek seeds and grind coarsely.
Mix mango with spices and salt.
Heat mustard oil till smoking, cool slightly, and pour over pickle.
Store in a clean jar for 7–10 days before use.

Lemon Pickle

Ingredients: Lemons (10), Salt (3 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~35 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Wash and cut lemons into small pieces.
Mix with salt and keep in sunlight for 4–5 days.
Add chili powder, turmeric, and mustard seeds.
Heat oil, let it cool, then mix with lemon pieces.
Store in a jar and use after 1 week.

Green Chili Pickle

Ingredients: Green chilies (250g), Mustard seeds (2 tbsp), Fennel seeds (1 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Mustard oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~30 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Wash and dry green chilies, slit them lengthwise.
Roast and grind mustard + fennel seeds.
Mix with salt and turmeric.
Stuff the masala into chilies.
Heat oil, cool slightly, and pour over chilies.
Keep in a jar for 3–4 days before serving.

Garlic Pickle

Ingredients: Garlic cloves (200g), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Fenugreek seeds (1 tsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Tamarind pulp (2 tbsp), Oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~45 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Peel garlic cloves.
Roast mustard and fenugreek seeds, grind them.
Heat oil, fry garlic till golden.
Add chili powder, salt, and tamarind pulp.
Mix spices and pour into a jar.
Store for 5–6 days before serving.

Amla Pickle

Ingredients: Amla (Indian gooseberry, 500g), Salt (3 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Mustard oil (½ cup), Fennel seeds (1 tbsp).

Calories: ~40 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Wash and steam amla for 3–4 min, then deseed.
Mix amla pieces with salt, turmeric, and chili powder.
Roast fennel seeds and grind lightly.
Heat mustard oil till smoking, cool slightly, and pour over amla.
Keep in a jar for 1 week before serving.

Carrot Pickle

Ingredients: Carrots (300g), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Fennel seeds (1 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Mustard oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~25 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Wash and cut carrots into thin sticks.
Dry them in sunlight for 2–3 hours.
Roast mustard + fennel seeds, grind coarsely.
Mix carrots with spices, salt, and turmeric.
Heat mustard oil till smoking, cool and pour over carrots.
Store for 3–4 days before serving.

Mixed Veg Pickle

Ingredients: Carrots (100g), Cauliflower (100g), Beans (100g), Peas (50g), Salt (3 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Mustard oil (½ cup).

Calories: ~30 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Cut all vegetables into small pieces.
Boil them slightly in hot water and drain completely.
Mix with salt, chili powder, and turmeric.
Heat mustard oil till smoking, then cool slightly.
Pour oil over vegetables and store in a clean jar.
Ready to eat after 5 days.

Ginger Pickle

Ingredients: Ginger (200g), Tamarind pulp (2 tbsp), Jaggery (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~40 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Wash, peel, and grate ginger.
Heat oil and fry ginger lightly.
Add tamarind pulp, jaggery, chili powder, and salt.
Cook for 5–6 minutes until thick.
Cool and store in a glass jar.
Can be eaten instantly.

Onion Pickle

Ingredients: Small onions (250g), Vinegar (½ cup), Salt (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (1 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp).

Calories: ~20 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Peel small onions and wash them.
Mix vinegar, salt, chili powder, and mustard seeds.
Add onions into the vinegar mixture.
Store in a jar for 3–4 days.
Pickle will be ready to eat.

Cabbage Pickle

Ingredients: Cabbage (200g, finely shredded), Salt (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Mustard oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~22 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Shred cabbage and dry in sunlight for 2 hours.
Mix with salt, turmeric, and chili powder.
Roast mustard seeds and add.
Heat mustard oil, cool, and pour over cabbage.
Store in a jar for 3–4 days before serving.

Turnip Pickle

Ingredients: Turnip (250g, sliced), Carrot (100g), Ginger (20g), Salt (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Vinegar (½ cup), Mustard oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~25 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Slice turnip and carrot, blanch for 2 min in hot water.
Dry completely in sunlight.
Mix with ginger, salt, chili powder, and vinegar.
Heat mustard oil, cool, and pour over mixture.
Store for 5–6 days, then serve.

Beetroot Pickle

Ingredients: Beetroot (200g, sliced), Vinegar (½ cup), Sugar (1 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Oil (2 tbsp).

Calories: ~28 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Slice beetroot thin and steam lightly.
Mix with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
Heat oil, add mustard seeds, and pour over beetroot.
Keep in a sterilized glass jar.
Ready after 3–4 days.

Cucumber Pickle

Ingredients: Cucumbers (200g, sliced), Vinegar (½ cup), Salt (2 tbsp), Sugar (2 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Chili flakes (1 tsp).

Calories: ~18 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Slice cucumbers thinly and sprinkle with salt.
Mix vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds, and chili flakes.
Add cucumber slices and stir well.
Store in refrigerator for 2–3 days.
Serve chilled.

Cauliflower Pickle

Ingredients: Cauliflower (200g florets), Carrot (50g), Salt (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Mustard oil (¼ cup).

Calories: ~26 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Wash and cut cauliflower into small florets.
Blanch in hot water for 2 min, then dry completely.
Mix with salt, turmeric, and chili powder.
Heat mustard oil, cool, and pour over mixture.
Store in airtight jar for 5–6 days.

Raw Turmeric Pickle

Ingredients: Fresh turmeric root (100g, julienned), Lemon juice (½ cup), Salt (1 tbsp), Green chilies (4, sliced), Mustard seeds (1 tsp).

Calories: ~15 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Wash and peel raw turmeric, cut into thin strips.
Add sliced green chilies and salt.
Pour lemon juice and mix well.
Add roasted mustard seeds for flavor.
Store in glass jar; ready in 2–3 days.

Radish Pickle

Ingredients: Radish (200g, sliced), Salt (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (1 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Mustard oil (¼ cup), Lemon juice (2 tbsp).

Calories: ~20 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Slice radish thin and sun-dry for 1–2 hrs.
Mix with salt, chili powder, and lemon juice.
Heat mustard oil, add mustard seeds.
Pour oil mix over radish slices.
Store in airtight jar for 3–4 days.

Bamboo Shoot Pickle

Ingredients: Bamboo shoots (150g, sliced), Mustard oil (¼ cup), Garlic (5 cloves), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Turmeric (1 tsp).

Calories: ~30 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Boil bamboo shoots for 10 min to remove bitterness.
Dry completely before mixing.
Heat mustard oil, add garlic, turmeric, and chili powder.
Mix with bamboo shoots and salt.
Store in glass jar for 4–5 days before use.

Tomato Pickle

Ingredients: Ripe tomatoes (300g), Tamarind (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tbsp), Oil (¼ cup), Garlic (6 cloves).

Calories: ~25 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 30 min

Steps:
Chop tomatoes and cook until water reduces.
Add tamarind pulp, chili powder, and salt.
Heat oil, fry mustard seeds and garlic.
Mix with tomato paste.
Store in sterilized jar; stays fresh for 2 weeks.

Curry Leaf Pickle

Ingredients: Fresh curry leaves (1 cup), Tamarind (2 tbsp), Red chili powder (2 tbsp), Salt (2 tbsp), Mustard seeds (1 tsp), Oil (¼ cup), Garlic (4 cloves).

Calories: ~22 kcal/tbsp • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Wash and dry curry leaves, then roast lightly.
Grind with tamarind, chili powder, and salt.
Heat oil, fry mustard seeds and garlic.
Mix with curry leaf paste.
Store in airtight jar; lasts 2–3 weeks.

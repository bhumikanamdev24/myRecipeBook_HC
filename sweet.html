Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: Khoya (1 cup), Maida (2 tbsp), Baking soda (pinch), Sugar (1½ cup), Water, Rose essence, Oil/Ghee.

Calories: ~150 kcal/piece • Prep: 40 min

Steps:
Khoya, maida aur baking soda ko mila kar soft dough banao.
Chhote balls banao aur golden brown hone tak oil/ghee me fry karo.
Sugar aur paani se syrup banao, rose essence daalo.
Fried gulab jamun ko syrup me soak karo 1–2 ghante.
Garma garam ya thande serve karo.

Rasgulla

Ingredients: Full cream milk (1 liter), Lemon juice, Sugar (1 cup), Water (3 cups), Cardamom.

Calories: ~120 kcal/piece • Prep: 50 min

Steps:
Milk ko boil karke lemon juice daalo aur paneer (chhena) banao.
Paneer ko knead karke smooth dough banao, chhote balls banao.
Water + sugar + cardamom ka syrup banao.
Balls ko syrup me daal kar 20–25 min cook karo jab tak fluffy na ho jaye.
Thoda thanda karke serve karo.

Jalebi

Ingredients: Maida (1 cup), Curd (2 tbsp), Baking soda (pinch), Sugar (1 cup), Water, Saffron, Oil.

Calories: ~150 kcal/2 pcs • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Maida, curd aur thoda paani dal kar batter banao, 8–10 hrs ferment hone do.
Sugar syrup me saffron daal kar ready rakho.
Batter ko cloth/piping bag me bhar ke hot oil me circular shapes banao.
Golden hone par syrup me dip karo.
Garma garam serve karo.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients: Cashews (1 cup), Sugar (½ cup), Water, Ghee, Cardamom.

Calories: ~100 kcal/piece • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Cashews ko grind karke powder banao.
Sugar aur paani ka syrup banao (1 string consistency).
Cashew powder daal kar ghee aur cardamom ke saath mix karo.
Dough ko rolling board par spread karke diamond shape cut karo.
Kaju katli thanda karke serve karo.

Rasmalai

Ingredients: Rasgulla (10 pcs), Full cream milk (1 liter), Sugar (½ cup), Saffron, Cardamom, Pistachio/Almond.

Calories: ~180 kcal/piece • Prep: 45 min

Steps:
Milk ko boil karke thoda thick karo, sugar + saffron + cardamom daalo.
Rasgulle ko squeeze karke milk me daal do.
15–20 min simmer karo taki milk flavors absorb ho jaye.
Dry fruits sprinkle karke thanda/thanda serve karo.

Rabri

Ingredients: Full cream milk (1 liter), Sugar (½ cup), Saffron, Cardamom, Almonds, Pistachio.

Calories: ~250 kcal/100g • Prep: 1 hr

Steps:
Milk ko slow flame pe boil karke thoda-thoda scrape karke reduce karo.
Sugar, saffron aur cardamom powder daalo.
Jab milk 1/3 ho jaye tab dry fruits daal kar mix karo.
Thanda karke serve karo ya garam bhi enjoy kar sakte ho.

Calories: ~250 kcal/bowl • Prep: 45 min

Garma garam ya chilled dono tarah enjoy kar sakte ho.

Ghee ke saath garma garam serve karo.

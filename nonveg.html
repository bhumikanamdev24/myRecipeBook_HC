Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken (500g), Butter (3 tbsp), Cream (4 tbsp), Tomato (3–4), Onion (2), Garam masala, Ginger-garlic paste, Salt.

Calories: ~490 kcal/serving • Prep: 45 min

Steps:
Chicken ko dahi + masale se 30 min marinate karo.
Onion + tomato gravy bana kar smooth paste karo.
Butter garam karke gravy cook karo aur cream daalo.
Chicken add karke 15 min dhimi aanch pe pakao.
Garam masala + fresh cream garnish karke serve karo.

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice (2 cups), Chicken (500g), Onion (2), Tomato (2), Yogurt, Spices, Ghee.

Calories: ~650 kcal/plate • Prep: 1 hr

Steps:
Rice ko 70% tak boil karo.
Chicken ko dahi + masale me 30 min marinate karo.
Onion ko fry karke golden bana lo.
Ek handi me rice aur chicken ke layers banao.
Dum pe 20 min slow cook karo aur raita ke saath serve karo.

Mutton Rogan Josh

Ingredients: Mutton (500g), Yogurt (½ cup), Kashmiri red chili, Onion, Ginger-garlic paste, Whole spices.

Calories: ~550 kcal/serving • Prep: 1 hr

Steps:
Mutton ko dahi + masale me marinate karo.
Onion + ginger-garlic fry karke masala banao.
Marinated mutton add karke slow cook karo.
Kashmiri mirch se laal rang aayega.
Garam masala daal kar naan ke saath serve karo.

Fish Curry

Ingredients: Fish pieces (400g), Onion (1), Tomato (2), Mustard seeds, Curry leaves, Tamarind.

Calories: ~320 kcal/serving • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Fish ko haldi + namak se coat karo.
Onion + tomato masala bana lo.
Tamarind pulp aur curry leaves add karo.
Fish gently gravy me daal kar 10 min pakao.
Rice ke saath serve karo.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients: Chicken boneless (400g), Yogurt (½ cup), Tomato puree (1 cup), Cream (3 tbsp), Ginger-garlic paste, Spices.

Calories: ~480 kcal/serving • Prep: 40 min

Steps:
Chicken cubes ko dahi + masale me marinate karo.
Tandoor/oven me grill karke tikka bana lo.
Tomato-based gravy cook karo.
Grilled tikka gravy me add karo aur cream daal do.
Roti/naan ke saath serve karo.

Egg Curry

Ingredients: Boiled eggs (4), Onion, Tomato, Ginger-Garlic paste, Masala, Oil.

Calories: ~210 kcal/bowl • Prep: 30 min

Steps:
Onion aur tomato ko fry karke masala gravy banao.
Ginger-garlic paste, mirch, haldi, dhania powder daalo.
Thoda paani daal kar gravy banne do.
Boiled eggs ko slit karke gravy me daal do.
5 min simmer karke serve karo with rice/roti.

Prawn Masala

Ingredients: Prawns (250g), Onion, Tomato, Ginger-Garlic paste, Spices, Oil.

Calories: ~180 kcal/serving • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Prawns ko wash karke haldi aur namak lagao.
Onion-tomato gravy banakar masala ready karo.
Usme prawns daal kar 8–10 min medium flame pe pakao.
Dhaniya patta garnish karo.
Garma garam prawns masala serve karo with roti/rice.

Tandoori Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken leg pieces, Yogurt, Lemon juice, Ginger-Garlic paste, Red chili powder, Garam masala.

Calories: ~260 kcal/piece • Prep: 45 min

Steps:
Chicken par cuts lagao aur dahi-masala marinade lagao.
Kam se kam 1 ghanta (better overnight) marinate karo.
Tandoor/oven me 200°C par 25–30 min bake karo.
Thoda butter brush karo.
Chat masala sprinkle karke serve karo.

Keema Matar

Ingredients: Mutton keema (300g), Green peas, Onion, Tomato, Ginger-Garlic paste, Spices, Oil.

Calories: ~320 kcal/bowl • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Onion golden fry karo aur usme tomato, ginger-garlic paste daalo.
Keema daal kar roast karo jab tak color change ho jaye.
Mirch, haldi, dhania powder aur namak daalo.
Matar daal kar paani add karo aur 20 min cook karo.
Fresh coriander garnish karke garma garam serve karo.

Mutton Korma

Ingredients: Mutton (500g), Yogurt, Onion, Cashew paste, Ginger-Garlic paste, Spices, Ghee.

Calories: ~400 kcal/bowl • Prep: 45 min

Steps:
Onion ko golden brown fry karke paste banao.
Mutton ko yogurt, ginger-garlic aur masalon me marinate karo.
Ghee me mutton roast karo aur onion paste + cashew paste daalo.
Thoda paani daal kar dhak kar 40 min slow cook karo.
Garam masala sprinkle karke serve karo.

Mutton Keema Paratha

Ingredients: Wheat flour (2 cups), Mutton keema (200g), Onion, Green chili, Ginger-Garlic paste, Spices, Oil/Ghee.

Calories: ~350 kcal/paratha • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Keema ko onion, green chili, ginger-garlic aur masalon ke saath fry karke stuffing banao.
Wheat flour ka dough banao.

Tawa par tel/ghee lagakar golden brown sekho.
Dahi ya achar ke saath serve karo.

Chicken Seekh Kebab

Ingredients: Chicken mince (300g), Onion, Green chili, Coriander leaves, Spices, Oil/Ghee.

Calories: ~250 kcal/2 kebabs • Prep: 30 min

Steps:

Mint chutney aur onion rings ke saath serve karo.

Mutton Handi

Ingredients: Mutton (500g), Onion, Tomato, Yogurt, Cashew paste, Ginger-Garlic paste, Spices, Ghee.

Calories: ~420 kcal/bowl • Prep: 50 min

Steps:
Onion fry karo aur tomato + ginger-garlic paste daalo.
Mutton ko yogurt aur masalon ke saath handi me cook karo.
Cashew paste aur thoda paani daal kar slow cook karo 40–45 min.
Jab mutton soft ho jaye, garam masala daal kar mix karo.
Ghee drizzle karke serve karo.

Egg Bhurji

Ingredients: Eggs (3–4), Onion, Tomato, Green chili, Spices, Oil/Butter.

Calories: ~200 kcal/serving • Prep: 15 min

Steps:
Onion aur green chili ko oil/butter me fry karo.
Tomato aur masale daal kar bhun lo.
Eggs tod kar directly pan me daalo aur scramble karo.
Cook until fluffy aur dhania se garnish karo.
Pav ya roti ke saath serve karo.

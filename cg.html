Chana Samosa

Ingredients: Boiled chana (1 cup), Boiled potato (2), Onion, Green chili, Spices, Oil, Samosa patti.

Calories: ~180 kcal/piece • Prep: 40 min

Steps:
Potatoes mash karo aur chana, onion, chili, spices mix karo.
Samosa pattis ya dough cone bana ke stuffing bharo.
Kinare paani se seal karo aur medium flame pe fry karo.
Golden brown hone par chutney ke saath serve karo.

Dubki Kadi

Ingredients: Besan (1 cup), Curd (1 cup), Curry leaves, Mustard seeds, Spices, Oil.

Calories: ~150 kcal/bowl • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Besan + curd + water ka smooth batter banao.
Salt, haldi, mirchi daal ke mix karo.
Oil me mustard seeds, curry leaves ka tadka lagao.
Batter add karke 8–10 min dhimi aanch pe pakaao.
Garam rice ke saath serve karo.

Fara (Rice Dumplings)

Ingredients: Rice flour (2 cups), Chana dal (1 cup), Garlic, Ginger, Chili, Oil.

Calories: ~120 kcal/piece • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Rice flour + hot water se soft dough banao.
Dal paste me ginger, garlic, chili mix karo.
Dough se small puri banakar dal stuffing bharo.
Fold karke steam karo 10–15 min.
Curry leaves ke tadke ke saath serve karo.

Chousela Roti

Calories: ~90 kcal/roti • Prep: 20 min

Steps:

Small balls banakar roti shape me bel lo.
Tawa par roast karo dono side till light brown.
Chutney ya curry ke saath serve karo.

Angakar Roti

Thethari

Ingredients: Rice flour (2 cups), Ajwain (½ tsp), Salt, Oil.

Calories: ~70 kcal/piece • Prep: 25 min

Steps:
Rice flour me ajwain aur namak daal kar stiff dough banao.
Dough ko rolling board par patla roll karo aur strips cut karo.
Oil garam karke medium flame par golden hone tak fry karo.
Thanda hone par airtight container me store karo.

Khurma

Ingredients: Maida (1 cup), Ghee (2 tbsp), Sugar (½ cup), Oil.

Calories: ~250 kcal/serving • Prep: 30 min

Steps:
Maida + ghee + thoda paani se stiff dough banao.
Dough ko chhoti chhoti diamonds shape me cut karo.
Medium flame pe fry karo jab tak golden ho jaye.
Sugar syrup bana kar fried pieces usme coat karo.
Thanda hone ke baad crispy khurma serve karo.

Tilgur Ladoo

Ingredients: Sesame seeds (1 cup), Jaggery (1 cup), Ghee (1 tsp).

Calories: ~120 kcal/ladoo • Prep: 20 min

Steps:
Sesame seeds ko dry roast karo jab tak light golden ho jaye.
Ek pan me jaggery melt karo aur ghee daal kar syrup banao.
Roasted til ko syrup me daal kar achchhe se mix karo.
Garam mixture ko jaldi jaldi chhote ladoo shape me roll karo.
Thoda thanda hone par serve karo.

Bara (Vada)

Ingredients: Urad dal (1 cup, soaked), Onion (1), Green chili (2), Oil, Salt.

Calories: ~150 kcal/vada • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Urad dal ko 4–5 ghante bhigo kar thick paste grind karo.
Onion, green chili aur namak dal kar mix karo.
Hath se ya spoon se shape dekar garam oil me fry karo.
Dono side golden hone tak fry karo.
Chutney ke saath garma garam serve karo.

Moong Dal Halwa

Ingredients: Moong dal (1 cup, soaked & ground), Ghee (4 tbsp), Sugar (½ cup), Milk (1 cup), Dry fruits.

Calories: ~300 kcal/bowl • Prep: 40 min

Steps:
Moong dal paste ko ghee me golden brown hone tak roast karo.
Sugar aur milk dal kar continuously stir karo.
Mixture thick aur aromatic hone lage tab tak pakaao.
Dry fruits dal kar mix karo.
Garam halwa bowl me serve karo.

Aamat (Veg Curry)

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, brinjal, drumstick), Tamarind pulp, Mustard seeds, Curry leaves, Oil.

Calories: ~180 kcal/bowl • Prep: 35 min

Steps:
Vegetables ko chop karke thoda boil karo.
Pan me oil garam karke mustard seeds & curry leaves daalo.
Tamarind pulp aur masala (haldi, mirchi) daal kar mix karo.
Boiled vegetables daal kar ache se pakaao.
Garam aamat ko chawal ke saath serve karo.

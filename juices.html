Orange Juice

Ingredients: Fresh oranges (4–5), Sugar (optional), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~110 kcal/glass • Prep: 10 min

Steps:
Peel the oranges and extract the juice using a juicer or by hand.
Add sugar if needed, but natural taste is best without sugar.
Pour into a glass, add ice cubes and serve chilled.

Apple Juice

Ingredients: Apples (2–3), Lemon juice (1 tsp), Sugar (optional), Ice.

Calories: ~120 kcal/glass • Prep: 8 min

Steps:
Wash and cut apples into small pieces.
Blend them in a juicer or mixer and strain the juice.
Add lemon juice to prevent discoloration.
Serve fresh with ice cubes.

Pineapple Juice

Ingredients: Pineapple chunks (2 cups), Sugar (optional), Water, Ice cubes.

Calories: ~130 kcal/glass • Prep: 10 min

Steps:
Peel and cut pineapple into chunks.
Blend with water until smooth and strain if needed.
Add sugar if the pineapple is sour.
Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Watermelon Juice

Ingredients: Watermelon cubes (3 cups), Mint leaves (few), Lemon juice (1 tsp), Ice.

Calories: ~80 kcal/glass • Prep: 5 min

Steps:
Remove seeds from watermelon and cut into cubes.
Blend the cubes into juice without adding extra water.
Add lemon juice and mint leaves for freshness.
Serve cold with ice cubes.

Mango Shake

Ingredients: Mango pulp (1 cup), Chilled milk (1 cup), Sugar (2 tbsp), Ice cream (optional).

Calories: ~200 kcal/glass • Prep: 7 min

Steps:
Blend mango pulp, chilled milk, and sugar until smooth.
Adjust sweetness as per taste.
Pour into a tall glass, top with ice cream if desired.
Serve chilled.

Banana Shake

Ingredients: Ripe bananas (2), Chilled milk (1½ cups), Sugar (2 tbsp), Ice cream (optional).

Calories: ~210 kcal/glass • Prep: 7 min

Steps:
Peel bananas and cut into slices.
Blend with chilled milk and sugar until smooth.
Pour into a tall glass and add ice cream if desired.
Serve chilled.

Pomegranate Juice

Ingredients: Pomegranate seeds (2 cups), Sugar (optional), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~130 kcal/glass • Prep: 8 min

Steps:
Separate the seeds from pomegranate.
Blend and strain the juice to remove pulp.
Add sugar if needed.
Serve fresh with ice cubes.

Grape Juice

Ingredients: Grapes (2 cups), Sugar (optional), Lemon juice (1 tsp), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~120 kcal/glass • Prep: 6 min

Steps:
Wash grapes thoroughly and blend them.
Strain to remove seeds and skin.
Add lemon juice and sugar if needed.
Serve cold with ice cubes.

Papaya Juice

Ingredients: Ripe papaya cubes (2 cups), Sugar (2 tbsp), Chilled water/milk (1 cup), Ice.

Calories: ~140 kcal/glass • Prep: 6 min

Steps:
Cut ripe papaya into small cubes.
Blend with chilled water or milk and sugar.
Pour into a glass and add ice cubes.
Serve fresh and chilled.

Guava Juice

Ingredients: Ripe guavas (2–3), Sugar (optional), Black salt (pinch), Water, Ice.

Calories: ~110 kcal/glass • Prep: 7 min

Steps:
Wash and cut guavas into pieces.
Blend with water and strain to remove seeds.
Add sugar and black salt for taste.
Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Lemon Juice

Ingredients: Fresh lemon (2), Sugar (2 tbsp), Salt (pinch), Chilled water (2 cups), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~40 kcal/glass • Prep: 5 min

Steps:
Squeeze fresh lemons into a bowl.
Mix sugar, salt, and chilled water.
Stir well until sugar dissolves.
Pour into a glass and add ice cubes.
Serve cold.

Kiwi Juice

Ingredients: Kiwi fruits (3), Sugar or honey (2 tbsp), Chilled water (1 cup), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~100 kcal/glass • Prep: 7 min

Steps:
Peel and chop kiwi into small pieces.
Blend with chilled water and sugar/honey.
Strain if needed.
Serve chilled with ice cubes.

Strawberry Shake

Ingredients: Fresh strawberries (1 cup), Milk (1½ cups), Sugar (2 tbsp), Ice cream (optional).

Calories: ~180 kcal/glass • Prep: 6 min

Steps:
Wash and chop strawberries.
Blend with milk and sugar until smooth.

Chikoo Shake

Ingredients: Ripe chikoo (sapota) (3–4), Chilled milk (1½ cups), Sugar (2 tbsp), Ice cubes.

Calories: ~200 kcal/glass • Prep: 6 min

Steps:
Peel and deseed chikoo fruits.

Coconut Water

Ingredients: Fresh tender coconut (1), Ice cubes (optional).

Calories: ~45 kcal/glass • Prep: 2 min

Steps:
Cut open a fresh tender coconut carefully.
Pour the coconut water into a glass.
Add ice cubes if you like chilled.
Serve fresh and natural.

Carrot Juice

Ingredients: Fresh carrots (3–4), Lemon juice (1 tsp), Honey (1 tbsp), Chilled water (½ cup).

Calories: ~80 kcal/glass • Prep: 8 min

Steps:
Wash and peel carrots, chop into pieces.
Blend with water until smooth.
Add honey and lemon juice.
Strain if desired, pour into glass.
Serve chilled.

Beetroot Juice

Ingredients: Beetroot (2), Carrot (1), Lemon juice (1 tsp), Ginger (small piece), Honey (optional).

Calories: ~90 kcal/glass • Prep: 10 min

Steps:
Wash, peel, and chop beetroot and carrot.
Blend with ginger and little water.
Add lemon juice and honey if required.
Strain and serve fresh.
